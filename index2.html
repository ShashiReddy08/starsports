STAR SPORTS

Star Sports is an Indian TV network that broadcasts a variety of sports events, including cricket, kabaddi, and the Premier League.
Some of the sub-channels of Star Sports include:
Star Sports 1: The English channel
Star Sports 1 HD: The English HD channel
Star Sports 1 Hindi: The Hindi channel
Star Sports 1 HD Hindi: The Hindi HD channel
Star Sports 1 Kannada: The Kannada channel
Star Sports 1 Tamil: The Tamil channel
Star Sports 1 HD Tamil: The Tamil HD channel
Star Sports 1 Telugu: The Telugu channel
Star Sports 1 HD Telugu: The Telugu HD channel
Star Sports First: The Hindi-language channel
You can watch Star Sports on Tata Play and Disney+ Hotstar.

STAR MAA

Star Maa is a Telugu-language general entertainment television channel in India that airs reality shows, serials, and Telugu films.
is owned by Disney Star, a subsidiary of Star India, which is owned by The Walt Disney Company.
Here are some details about Star Maa:
History: Star Maa was launched in 2002 as Maa TV.
In 2015, Disney Star acquired the Maa bouquet of channels, and in 2017, Maa TV rebranded to Star Maa.
Competition: Star Maa competes with Zee Telugu, Gemini TV, and ETV. Availability: Star Maa is available on Disney+ Hotstar..

MOBILE BROADCASTER

Disney+ Hotstar is a streaming service that offers a variety of content, including movies, TV shows, sports, and more:
Content
Disney+ Hotstar offers a wide range of content, including Disney+ originals, Hollywood movies, Bollywood movies, regional shows, and live sports.
Languages
Disney+ Hotstar offers content in over 17 languages, including regional language dubs of Disney+ shows and movies.
Number of users
Disney+ Hotstar has over 300 million users.
Number of devices
Disney+ Hotstar Premium Plan subscribers can log in to up to 10 devices, but can only stream on up to 3 devices simultaneously.
Payment options
You can pay for a Disney+ Hotstar subscription using credit cards, debit cards, netbanking, UPI, Paytm Wallet, or iTunes.
Subscription plans
Disney+ Hotstar offers recurring monthly, quarterly, or annual subscription plans.
